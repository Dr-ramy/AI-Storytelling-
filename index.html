General objectives


-Develop AI-powered storytelling platforms capable of generating dynamic and immersive narratives that seamlessly blend human creativity with AI-generated elements.
-Explore the potential applications of AI technologies, such as natural language processing, machine learning, and generative algorithms, in augmenting and enhancing traditional storytelling techniques.
-Investigate the impact of AI-enhanced storytelling on student engagement, motivation, and learning outcomes across diverse educational settings and subject areas.
-Design user-friendly interfaces and authoring tools to empower educators and students to collaboratively design and customize AI-driven storytelling experiences according to their educational objectives and interests.
-Conduct empirical research to assess the effectiveness of AI-generated storytelling interventions in promoting knowledge acquisition, retention, and transfer, as well as developing higher-order thinking skills such as creativity, problem-solving, and critical analysis. ...
-Explore innovative methods for integrating AI-generated characters, plot twists, and story branches into existing educational curricula and instructional practices to enhance learning effectiveness and relevance.
-Develop guidelines and best practices for ethical storytelling practices, including considerations of algorithmic bias, cultural sensitivity, and privacy concerns, to ensure the responsible and inclusive use of AI technologies in educational contexts.
-Continuously iterate and refine AI-driven storytelling technologies and methodologies based on feedback from educators and student.
